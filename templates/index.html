About us


Hope for Homeless is a nonprofitable organisation founded in 2023 by team Runtime Error. Homelessness can strike families and individuals for a number of reasons. Often times it is a mix of low wages and sudden calamity Our website links homeless individuals with housing, job training programs and employment opportunities to help connect individuals with the resources they need to find and maintain a job. This includes information about job training programs, job fairs, and other resources that can help individuals build their skills and find employment.
